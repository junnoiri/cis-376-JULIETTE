Bad UI Example

One day, I tried to register for the next semester on my smartphone. However, the screen below was displayed even then, and I gave up.

The screen looked fine when I just opened it.

However, when I tried to register a class, I really couldn't figure out where the button to search for new classes was.(Actually, it was a button labeled "Panel" on the bottom left of the screen.)

I finally found the button, but once I clicked on the link that said Advanced Search, the UI collapsed as the search form covered the original text.

This could lead to users registering the wrong class, or at least frustration. This is not a great UI from a mobile usability perspective.

Of course, most students, myself included, usually use laptops to register for classes. However, if it can be easily registered through a smartphone, it will be very convenient.

So I fixed the problems and simplified the search form
